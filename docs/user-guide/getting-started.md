# Getting Started with AI Road Trip Storyteller

Welcome to your new travel companion! This guide will help you get up and running with the AI Road Trip Storyteller in just a few minutes.

## Quick Start

### 1. Download the App
- **iOS**: Available on the App Store
- **Android**: Available on Google Play Store

### 2. Create Your Account
1. Open the app and tap "Get Started"
2. Enter your email and create a password
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete your profile with travel preferences

### 3. Grant Permissions
For the best experience, allow these permissions:
- **Location**: Required for navigation and location-based stories
- **Microphone**: Required for voice commands
- **Notifications**: Optional, for booking confirmations and updates

### 4. Take Your First Trip!
1. Tap the "New Journey" button
2. Enter your destination or say "Hey Roadtrip, let's go to [destination]"
3. Select your journey type (family trip, concert, business, etc.)
4. Hit the road and enjoy your personalized experience!

## Voice Activation

Activate your AI companion with these wake phrases:
- "Hey Roadtrip"
- "OK Storyteller"
- "Hello Navigator"

## First-Time Setup Tips

### Connect Your Music
- Link Spotify for personalized road trip playlists
- The app will match music to your journey mood

### Set Your Preferences
- Choose your preferred voice personality
- Set story frequency (more stories vs. quiet time)
- Configure family-friendly content filters

### Enable Offline Mode
- Download maps for your route
- Cache stories for areas with poor connectivity
- Save up to 5 routes offline

## Safety First

The app automatically:
- Pauses during complex driving situations
- Simplifies commands at high speeds
- Provides hands-free operation
- Offers large-button mode for passengers

## Need Help?

- Say "Hey Roadtrip, help me" for voice assistance
- Tap the help icon (?) for context-sensitive guidance
- Visit our support portal at roadtripstoryteller.com/support
